Top three places to visit in Charlton

Visit Buffumville Lake

Buffumville lake is a premier spot for water sports, swimming, and hiking!

Visit Fay Mountain Farm

A historic farm kept in use by the town. Enjoy scenic walks among the fruit and vegetables!

Dexter Memorial

Visit the historic civil war monument dedicated to Union soldiers who took part in the war.
